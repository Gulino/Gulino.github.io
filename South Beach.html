The Art Deco District

Here we are at the most iconic neighbourhood in Greater Miami, South Beach! Its heart is the Art Deco Historic District, from 18th St and south long Ocean Drive and Collins Avenue.This unique architectural complex has made Miami famous all over the world, even if back in the days South Beach was an unsafe place, devoted to drugs traffic and crime, like it has been shown by famous tv shows like Miami Vice.
Thanks to theMiami Design Preservation League (MDPL) the Art Deco district has been restored becoming one of the largest areas in the USA on the National Register of Historic Places.
If you want to know more about SoBe art deco buildings, an amazing introduction is the guided walking tour of the art deco district organized by the Miami Design Preservation League: no reservation is required, and for only 20 dollars you will have a two hour walk around the most beautiful and well-preserved art deco architectures in the world, like Carlyle Hotel, The Colony, Cavalier South Beach, Delano Hotel and many others.

The Bass Museum of Art

South Beach is not only white sand, palms, classic cars and models, one of my favourite cultural spot in there isThe Bass , the best art museum in Miami Beach.
Its permanent collection is huge, with highlights range from 16th century European works to Renaissance paintings.
Besides the collection, the Bass is well known for its contemporary arts exhibitions of international mid-career and established artists.
